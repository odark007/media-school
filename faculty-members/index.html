Dr. Kofi Osei-Kusi Vice-Chancellor
Dr. Osei Kusi is a life coach, a speaker, a consultant and a family man. He is passionate about the youth. He serves as the execute president of the Osei Kusi Foundation.

He has over 18 years of experience in working with young people. Many of his mentees are among business owners, middle to senior managers, leading thinkers and church leers.

He is the author of 14 books on wisdom; a columnist for the business and financial Times Newspaper , and a regular guest on TV and radio programs.

Mr Leslie Addo Listowell Rector
Mr Leslie Listowell is a prolific and dynamic communication personal, a trained marketer and an entrepreneur with over eight (8) years of working experience in different roles. He holds a Higher Diploma in Marketing from Accra Polytechnic, now Accra Technical University and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Marketing from the Central University College. Mr Listowell took a TV presenting course at the Australian Media college through distance learning.

Mr Listowell is the CEO of Media Tribe studio 233, a multimedia company that produces content for several TV stations in Ghana. Hitherto, he worked with NET2 television as a Marketing executive and moved to Crystal Television. He was once the media manager for the Elite Kingdom for 4 years. He also anchored documentary channels on NET2 in the late 90’s and then TV market on crystal TV, Mezzo Maths on HomeBase tv, kiddie tym on GHOne tv, R13 on GHOne tv and Metro TV, Campus fylla on Joy Prime.

Mr Listowell is the chief executive officer of the Gh School, mother brand of the popular GH Media,GH Cosmetology and GH Fashion schools. He is also the Rector and lecturer for TV Presenting.

Mr. Clifford Ladzekpo Deputy Rector Academic Director
Mr Clifford Ladzekpo is an astute academician and hands-on multimedia expert. He has over 28 years of working experience in Television and Broadcast. He worked with the then Ghana Film Industry Corporation as a camera assistant and moved to become the head of production and editorial for the evergreen living Word Broadcast by Pastor Mensa Otabil, a position he played creditably over a decade. Mr. Ladzekpo holds a Master of Philosophy degree in Public Health and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication Studies.

He also doubles as a life coach and counsellor: building and transforming lives and destinies. He has a strong technical background in electronics with guilds of UK qualifications and computer hardware. Mr. Ladzekpo also has international training and exposure in Europe and the USA. He is an alumnus and member of the National Association of Broadcasters, the organizers of the biggest electronic media conference and exhibition held annually in Las Vegas, USA.

Mr. Ladzekpo joined GH Media in its inception stage in 2014 as Lecturer and has risen through the ranks of the head of department, head of School, management member and now Deputy Rector in charge of Academics.

Mrs. Belomina Addo Listowell Deputy Rector - Monitoring and Supervision
Mrs. Belomina Addo Listowell is an educationist with a Master of Arts Degree in Administration in High Education from the University of Cape Coast.
