HOME FEE STRUCTURE

ADMISSION FEES

All admitted students are required to pay one-time a non-refundable admission fee to secure their admission. This fee is paid upon the receipt of the students admission letter, the admission fee must be paid within 14 days, upon receipt of the admission letter, to enable the student to secure the admission slot. Students are given student numbers to enable them proceed with their admission process.

SCHOOL FEES

Students will be required to pay 50% of their fees before registration, 70% of the school fees will be required before the middle of the semester, students are given 70% fee card and this enables them to write their mid-semester examinations. Before the end of the semester, students are required to pay 100% of their fees and are given FULL FEES CARDS this allows them to participate in the END OF SEMESTER EXAMINATION. Students without 70% FEES CARD and FULL FEES CARD are not allowed to write their mid-semester exams and end of semester exams respectively
